About Zero Gravity

Meet the team

Our team consists of 5 high school students. The team was created on early October 2014 and was meant to participate in the National F1 in schools competition in Cyprus. We represent our school "Livadia Larnaca High School".

National Finals

At the national finals we managed to win one of the first places which secured us a position at the World Finals. We also won 5 out of 8 individual awards.

World Finals

We are now going to participate in the world finals. This year's finals will be taking place in Singapore on 13-16 of September 2015. Students from all over the globe will be there representing their country, cultures, and customs. The competitiveness on a worldwide level is large but we will fight with all of our power. As "Zero Gravity" the team and as a Cypriot team, we aspire on a good place in the final classification in order to identify the island globally and to show our satisfaction to all those who supported us.
